Best Movies according to me

Here comes my favorites 5 movies

  1. The Golden Compass

    2. Orphan Lyra Belacqua lives in a parallel world where human souls manifest in animal form, and are called daemons.
    She discovers that several children are disappearing, including her best friend Roger, so she goes with Pantalaimon, her dæmon, in search of answers.
    With the help of an ancient instrument and friends she makes along the way, Lyra sets out on a journey that could change the world forever.

  2. Damsel

    3. A young woman agrees to marry a handsome prince, only to discover it was all a trap.
    She is thrown into a cave with a fire-breathing dragon and must rely solely on her wits and will to survive.

  3. Abominable

    4. A teenage girl and her friends embark on an epic journey to reunite a Yeti with its beloved family.
    To do this, they must reach the highest point on the planet.

  4. Avatar the Last Airbender

    5. The four nations, Air, Water, Earth and Fire, lived in harmony, until the Fire nation declared war with the aim of dominating the world.
    A century later, there is no end to the destruction, until an avatar named Aang discovers he has the power to control the four elements.
    He joins the brothers Katara and Sokka, from Water, and together they go on a great and dangerous adventure in search of peace, to restore balance and harmony.

  5. Wolf children

    6. Ōkami Kodomo no Ame to Yuki is a 2012 Japanese animated film directed by Mamoru Hosoda.
    The film stars the voices of Aoi Miyazaki, Takao Osawa, Haru Kuroki and Yukito Nishii.
    In Ame to Yuki, Hana falls in love with a werewolf.
    Together, they have two werewolf children: Yuki, the eldest, and Ame.
